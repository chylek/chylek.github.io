Reboot now

Works only with ROOTED devices.

Reboot - simple task, simple app.

From time to time your devices need a reboot. With use of other schedulers you can run this app at night to ensure your device reboots when you don't mind. Of course if you are using the device at that time, the app gives you enough time to cancel the reboot with one tap.

Please run this app manually for the first time to grant it the superuser rights.
